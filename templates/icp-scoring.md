# ICP Scoring Rubric

Customize these criteria and weights for your specific business. The Prospector agent uses this to score leads A/B/C.

## Scoring Criteria (100 points total)

### Industry Match (0-20 points)
- 20: Exact target industry (e.g., SaaS, agency, consulting)
- 15: Adjacent industry (e.g., tech services, professional services)
- 10: Broad match (e.g., B2B company)
- 0: Consumer, government, non-profit (unless that's your target)

### Revenue (0-20 points)
- 20: Sweet spot ($1M-$10M ARR — big enough to pay, small enough to need help)
- 15: Slightly outside ($500K-$1M or $10M-$50M)
- 10: Early stage (pre-revenue but funded)
- 5: Too large ($50M+ — long sales cycle)
- 0: Unknown/unfundable

### Headcount (0-15 points)
- 15: 10-100 employees (decision-maker accessible)
- 10: 100-500 employees (need to navigate org chart)
- 5: 500+ (enterprise sales cycle)
- 0: Solo founder or 1-5 (can't afford services)

### Tech Stack Signals (0-15 points)
- 15: Uses tools that indicate readiness (CRM, automation, AI tools)
- 10: Uses some relevant tools
- 5: Basic tech stack
- 0: No signals

### Decision-Maker Title (0-15 points)
- 15: C-suite (CEO, COO, CRO)
- 12: VP level (VP Sales, VP Ops, VP Marketing)
- 8: Director level
- 5: Manager level
- 0: IC or unclear

### Buying Signals (0-15 points)
- 15: Active signals (job posting for role you replace, funding round, competitor switch)
- 10: Passive signals (LinkedIn engagement with relevant content)
- 5: Weak signals (company growth, new office)
- 0: No signals

## Tier Thresholds

| Tier | Score | Channels | % of Pipeline |
|------|-------|----------|--------------|
| **A** | 70-100 | Email + Phone + SMS | Top 20% |
| **B** | 40-69 | Email + SMS | Middle 50% |
| **C** | 0-39 | Email only | Bottom 30% |

## Updating Thresholds

The Sequence Manager reviews tier performance weekly. If B-leads consistently convert as well as A-leads, the threshold should be lowered. If A-leads aren't converting, the criteria need tightening.
