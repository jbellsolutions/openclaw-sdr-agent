# Email Templates — Starter Set for A/B Testing

These are starting templates. The Email SDR agent will personalize each one and the weekly self-improvement loop will evolve them based on what converts.

## First Touch — Variant A (Pain Point Lead)

**Subject**: Quick question about [specific pain point]

Hi [First Name],

Noticed [Company] is [specific observation — hiring, launching, growing]. When companies hit that stage, [pain point] usually becomes a bottleneck.

We helped [similar company] [specific result] in [timeframe]. Curious if that's relevant?

Worth a 15-min call this week?

[Your Name]

## First Touch — Variant B (Social Proof Lead)

**Subject**: How [similar company] solved [problem]

Hi [First Name],

[Similar company] was spending [X hours/dollars] on [process] before they switched to [your solution]. Now they [result].

Would it make sense to see if we could do the same for [Company]?

[Your Name]

## Follow-Up Day 2

**Subject**: Re: [original subject]

Hi [First Name],

Quick follow-up — did my email land at a bad time?

The gist: we help companies like [Company] [one-line value prop].

If timing's better next week, happy to adjust.

[Your Name]

## Follow-Up Day 4

**Subject**: [First Name] — one more thought

Hi [First Name],

Last note from me. [One specific insight about their industry/company that shows you did research].

If [pain point] ever becomes a priority, here's my calendar: [link]

[Your Name]

## Breakup Email Day 7

**Subject**: Should I close your file?

Hi [First Name],

I've reached out a few times and haven't heard back — totally fine, I know you're busy.

I'll assume the timing isn't right and close this out. If anything changes, my inbox is always open.

Best,
[Your Name]

---

## A/B Testing Notes

- Test subject lines first (biggest impact on opens)
- Test opening lines second (biggest impact on replies)
- Only test ONE variable at a time
- Need 50+ sends per variant for statistical significance
- Winner = higher reply rate, not higher open rate (replies > opens)
